Privacy & Data

How PT Generator stores and uses your data.

What data is collected

  • Your Google account email and display name (via OAuth sign-in)
  • Client profiles: names, physical constraints, injury notes, equipment preferences, machine settings, trainer notes
  • Session history: exercise loads, sets/reps, session dates, coaching cues, progression notes
  • SMART fitness goals and milestone tracking
  • Trainer preferences: display name, gym name, contact info, bio
  • An append-only audit log of key actions (login, client create/edit/delete, session generate)

Where data is stored

All data is stored as JSON files on a Railway.app persistent volume attached to this service. There is no third-party database. Data is not encrypted at rest; access is controlled by your Railway account credentials and the Google OAuth login required to use this app.

What is sent to Claude (Anthropic)

When you generate a training plan, suggest a session focus, brainstorm goals, or request a progress summary, the following client data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API:

  • Client name, physical constraints, and injury notes
  • Preferred equipment and machine settings
  • Recent session history (loads, dates, progression notes)
  • Active fitness goals

Anthropic's data usage is governed by their Privacy Policy. API calls are not used to train Claude models under their standard API terms.

Your rights

  • Export: Download a complete ZIP of all your data at any time via the button below.
  • Delete: Delete individual clients from the client detail page. Deleted clients are moved to a trash folder and permanently purged after 30 days.
  • Account removal: To remove your account and all associated data, delete all clients and contact the app administrator.
